Gabs

I work with photography, illustrations, digital software, and other physical mediums. I grab most of my inspiration from other conceptual contemporary artworks. I’m interested in how pieces engage viewers for some time by getting them to think and respond. My approach is defined by zoning in on matters happening in the now. Social and political discourses. Trends. I try for everything, but notice it leads to the mundane. I’ve explored documenting the regular moments of cats through digital photography, and am venturing more into the mediums of ceramics, web, and painting! As I write this, I’m still figuring out what and if I will have an art practice >_<. Join me in trusting the process, keeping the arts in my life. Or better yet, let’s see what I learn and what sticks for my future of my pracice.
