Hi, I’m Gabby

When I’m feeling creative I make visuals such as this one. I work with photography, illustrations, digital software, and other physical mediums. I grab most of my inspiration from conceptual artworks and other contemporary works. I’m interested in pieces that engage viewers for some time by getting them to think or react, and aren't just subjectively nice to look at; even though that’s also nice. My approach is defined by zoning in on matters happening in the now. Social and political discourses. Trends. Everything, and the mundane. As of recently, I’ve explored documenting the regular moments of cats through digital photography, and am venturing more into the mediums of ceramics, web, and painting! As I write this, I’m still figuring out what and if I will have an art practice. Join me in trusting the process, and keeping the arts in my life. Or let’s see what I learn and what sticks for the future of my work.
