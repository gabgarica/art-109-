Gabryelle Garcia

Hi, I’m Gabby

I work with photography, illustrations, digital software, and other physical mediums. I get most of my inspiration from conceptual contemporary artworks. I’m interested in how such works engage viewers for some time by getting them to think, feel, and respond. My approach is largely defined by zoning in on matters happening in the now. I notice it often leads to this theme of regularity and mundane-ness. I’ve explored documenting the nature of cats through digital photography and am venturing more into the mediums of ceramics, web, and painting! As I write this, I’m still figuring out what and if I will have an art practice in connection to my career choice later on >_<. Join me in trusting the art process of cultivating skills and using art as a form of expression and connection. Or better yet, let’s see what I learn and what sticks for my future self and practice.
